Every Child, One Voice

The PTA improves the school experience for your child and every child at Wheeler Elementary, by raising funds and recruiting volunteers to provide a variety of social activities, events, and extra resources for every teacher and classroom. Popular PTA-Sponsored activities include the Grand Guest Lunch for grandparents every September, the Walk-for-Wheeler to raise funds for extra classroom resources, and recruitment of Room Parents to help with special occasions in the classroom.
