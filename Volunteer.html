Become A Volunteer Today!

Wheeler Elementary PTA uses Sign-Up Genius to make it quick and easy to sign-up and manage volunteers. Sign-Up Genius is free. Simply create a user account and then select the volunteer opportunities that are of interest to you. You'll receive requests for specific events and reminders when you sign-up to volunteer at an event.

You may also contact the PTA via email at wheelerpta@hotmail.com to learn more about volunteer opportunities.

Volunteer Opportunities

The Wheeler PTA plans and conducts several activities and events to build the Wheeler Elementary school community. We need volunteers to make these things happen. Please consider volunteering.

Room Parent
work closely with your child's teacher to assist with classroom celebrations and other projects
Walk-a-thon
assist the Walk-a-thon chairperson by helping with activity stations on the day of the walk (late Sept.)
Luncheons
help greet lunch visitors, assist with parking, set-up and clean-up for Grand Guest Lunch, Superman Lunch and Wonder Woman lunch
Wheeler Workers
meet first Thursday of each month at 9:15 a.m. to assist teachers with clerical tasks, copies, stapling, cutting forms, etc.
Picture Day Volunteer
assist children, photographers and teachers in getting everyone in front of the camera in a timely way
Vision/Hearing Screening Volunteer
assist with getting students to and from vision/hearing testing
Membership
assist with membership drives
Yearbook
volunteers will assist in yearbook sales, taking pictures at events, etc. to create a wonderful book of memories for students
Field Day
assist with field day activities in late May
PTA Board and Committees
provide leadership in planning and developing resources for PTA activities
Other
If you have a talent/strength that you believe would benefit the school, please let us know.

All volunteers need to complete a background records check prior to volunteering. Click here for the JCPS Volunteer Records Check
