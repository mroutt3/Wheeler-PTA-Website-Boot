PTA Membership

Wheeler is fortunate to have an excellent Parent Teacher Association (PTA) that always provides supportive services and programs to our students. Everyone is invited and encouraged to join the PTA and to become a part of this very important key to our school success.

PTAs provide parents, teachers, and community members the opportunity to work together to improve the lives of children and youth through programs that address health, education, and social concerns.

PTA membership is open to all who want to join. All parents, teachers, administrators, students, grandparents and other interested members of the community are welcome. PTA dues are $5 per person.

PTA Membership Form

Membership Dues are $5 per person, and are for the school-year

Please send printed form and $5 per member to Wheeler Elementary. You may send in your child's backpack to the attention of their teacher, or mail to Wheeler Elementary PTA, 5700 Cynthia Drive, Louisville, KY 40291
